Open IronGauge

What it does

  • Training log — plan your split, log sets fast (per-set or all at once), and see weekly volume by day or by muscle group.
  • Nutrition — log meals against calorie and macro targets (including sugar, saturated fat and sodium limits), build reusable meal plans, and quick-add meals in plain text.
  • Body tracking — weigh-ins with a 7-day trend and goal projection.
  • Works offline — install it to your home screen and it runs like a normal app, no connection needed.

Privacy first

IronGauge has no accounts, no servers, no analytics and no ads. Everything you log is stored on your own device. You can optionally connect your own Google Drive for automatic backups — they go to a hidden app folder in your account that only IronGauge can see, and the developer never has access to anything. Details in the privacy policy.

Install

  • Android (Chrome): open the app → menu (⋮) → Add to Home screen / Install app.
  • iPhone (Safari): open the app → Share → Add to Home Screen.

Tip for iPhone users: iOS can clear a web app's saved data if it goes unused for a while — connecting Google Drive backup inside the app protects you from that.
